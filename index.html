Volpino Italiano Lovers

Welcome to the fan page of the best breed in the world, a love of my life, a dog breed called Volpino Italiano. Volpino Italiano is a rare spitz-type breed of dog originally from Italy. The name means literally "Italian fox". This page gives you a great overview of the breed and allows you to follow the latest Volpino Italiano related information and the upcoming dog shows and events.
